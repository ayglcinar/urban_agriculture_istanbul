Gathering of the Families

On November 17, 1982, families who lacked information on the whereabouts of their relatives gathered for the ﬁrst time at Corniche el Mazraa, Beirut. Hundreds of people, mostly women, gathered after the call of Wadad Halwani, whose husband Adnan, had been abducted.
